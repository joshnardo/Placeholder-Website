Vans - Sidestripe Sessions

Together with Vans, we helped develop Sidestripe Sessions, a live music series shot at Vans HQ in Costa Mesa CA. Over 2 years and 24 episodes, we hosted Anderson Paak, Idles, Cuco, Quicksand, Channel Tres, and more.

Tasty Heavy handled all aspects of Video and Audio Production and Post Production.
